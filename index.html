About RG Ventures Lighting

Premium decorative lighting with a precise architectural point of view.

RG Ventures Lighting is a Delhi-based lighting studio specializing in premium decorative lighting and custom fixtures.

From bespoke chandeliers to curated imported collections, we bring together craftsmanship and design to create statement lighting for modern spaces.

Our work has been featured in high-end hospitality and residential projects, including installations in premium hotels such as JW Marriott.

We focus on material quality, design precision, and lighting that enhances architectural spaces.
